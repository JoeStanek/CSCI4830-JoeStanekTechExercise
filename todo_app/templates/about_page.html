About this app.

This app is here to help me track any potential assignments, appointments, or important dates and more!

This app was made by Josef Stanek for the CSCI4380 class at UNO, with help from following Myoungkyu Song's contacts example.

Using this app you are able to:

  • View your list of tasks and search for any you've added
  • Add tasks to your todo list
  • Edit and/or remove those tasks from your list
